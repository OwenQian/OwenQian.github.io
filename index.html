About

Hi, I'm a computer engineering student at the University of Waterloo.

I intend to keep my website simple, as a no-frills way for readers to see my projects/code and get some insight to what I'm interested in.

I want to start off by saying that I enjoy software development because of what you can do with code. Many difficult puzzles and problems manifest themselves as coding challenges; it's the process of fitting together small components into abstraction layers and using them to create more and more complex systems that gets me excited about coding.

I've never been overly concerned with how many tools and frameworks I can claim to be proficient with on my resume for me (although I do realize that framework knowledge is important). What I've learned, I've learned through picking-up diving head first into projects that have interested me.
